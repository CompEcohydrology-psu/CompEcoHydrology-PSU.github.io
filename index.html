Computational Eco-hydrology @ Penn State




Cibin Raj is the PI of Computational Ecohydrology Group at Penn State. He holds a dual appointment in Civil and Environmental Engineering Depoartment and Agricultural and Biological Engineering Department.

CV Bio

Our group uses mathematical models for quantifying ecohydrological impacts of climate and land use change, deep learning application in water quality modeling, and development of decision support tools for sustainable watershed management.
